Background

I completed my Bachelor of Science at Monash University (Clayton, Victoria, Australia) in 2015, where I completed majors in Astrophysics and Earth Sciences. I then moved to the Australian National University (ANU) in the Research School of Astronomy and Astrophysics (RSAA), where I completed a Masters of Astronomy and Astrophysics (Advanced) in 2017. I studied towards a PhD following my Masters Degree.

I now work as a data scientist, as well as doing some science communication work on the side. My science communication includes writing pieces for the Canberra Times (sometimes co-published in the Hobart Examiner) and talking to groups about astronomy. If you're interested in getting in touch about any of my work, please contact me at eloisekb.contact@gmail.com.
